Step 1 of 3 · Mechanism 1 · addresses Challenge A

Before you share anything sensitive

This appears just before your first disclosure, not buried at sign-up. Layer one is short. Open layer two only if you want the detail.

In plain terms:
  • What you tell us is used to give you care and match you to the right therapist.
  • Some early steps can be AI-assisted. We always tell you when.
  • Your therapist (a human) reviews anything an AI suggests. You can talk to a human at any time.
  • Your health information is sensitive data. We only use it for your care, never to sell.
Show me exactly how my disclosure is used (Layer 2)

Lawful basis: your explicit consent (GDPR Article 9, special category health data). You can withdraw it at any time without losing access to a human therapist.

Where AI is involved: intake summarisation and an initial therapist match. AI does not diagnose and does not make the final clinical decision; a named human clinician does.

Retention & access: stored encrypted, accessible only to your care team. You can request a copy or deletion (a Subject Access Request) from Settings at any time.

Your control: you may decline AI-assisted steps below and still continue with a fully human journey.

No boxes are pre-ticked. Agreeing is an active choice.
Build note: consent gate fires before disclosure; layered (1 short + 1 detailed); explicit opt-in; AI-decline path preserved. Verifies constraint set "Mechanism 1".

Explainable match, with a one-click override
